Our Mission

Lil Movements is a community of conscious movement guided by Lily. Our mission is to bring people back to nature through dance, qigong, and yoga-inspired motion. We believe movement heals the mind, balances the body, and connects the soul no matter your age, ability, or background. Every class supports a greater cause, with monthly donations to local organizations helping children, women, and mental wellness centers.

Rooted in energy, breath, and rhythm.

"It's not about being better than someone else. It's about being better than before."

Classes

Classes are filmed outdoors at the beach, in the mountains, under the trees to remind us that nature moves with us. Each session blends yoga, qigong, and dance into simple techniques that awaken the liver, thyroid, and inner rhythm of your body. From gentle breathing to powerful motion, every video is a doorway to reconnecting with your energy.

Meet Lily

Lily is an artist, dancer, and founder of Lil Movements. Her work blends movement therapy, art, and spiritual wellness into an empowering experience for all bodies. Her classes were born in an art gallery in Santa Barbara a place where creativity and healing met for the first time. Today, Lil Movements continues that spirit: art in motion, energy in balance, and the joy of being alive.

Community
