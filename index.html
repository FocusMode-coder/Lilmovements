Our Mission

At Lil Movements we guide each person to reconnect with their energy through sequences that combine conscious movement, breath work and stretches inspired by qigong and dance. The invitation is to expand and contract, listen to the body and recover the center. The focus is consistency, enjoying the process and building real wellbeing regardless of the starting point.

Meet Lily

Lillian Hahn Shining is an artist, horsewoman, promoter of solidarity projects and mother. Trained in music and dance in New York, she developed a practice that integrates body awareness and presence.

Her journey unites stages, galleries and community, always with a clear intention: to move energy so that each person finds their best version.

"It's not about being better than someone else. It's about being better than before."
